Origin Story

Peter Parker

Bitten by a radioactive spider, high school student Peter Parker gained the speed, strength, and powers of a spider. When his beloved Uncle Ben was killed, Peter learned that with great power comes great responsibility.


Now he fights crime as the amazing Spider-Man, protecting New York City while balancing his dual life as a student, photographer, and superhero. The web-slinger swings through Manhattan's concrete canyons, forever dedicated to being the city's protector.
